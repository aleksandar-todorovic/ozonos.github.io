Simply beautiful

Atom, the desktop environment of Ozon is designed to not get into your way and and be simple, sleek and modern and focused on helping you get stuff done as fast as possible.

Gaming ready

If you're one of the folks that want to game on Linux, Ozon is the distro for you. It comes with performance optimisations and easy way to install video drivers and gaming platforms, so you will be able to play plenty of games with ease.

Open source

We strongly believe in open source and for that reason we've made Ozon completely open source. You can find the source on , contribute to it or if you feel so inclined - fork it. We would really appreciate any sort of contributions.
